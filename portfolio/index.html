This project, built using Python, Flask, HTML, and CSS, leverages AI to optimize energy consumption in smart homes. The system automates appliances based on occupancy patterns and energy demand, reducing wastage and lowering electricity costs. A web-based dashboard provides real-time monitoring, AI-driven insights, and cost-saving recommendations. By integrating renewable energy sources, this solution enhances energy efficiency, making homes smarter and more sustainable.
